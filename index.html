ABOUT US

We provide delivery services

This app focusses on getting an individual his/her desired products/items delivered to college by his/her fellow mate who is in city. The person who will depart from college and wants to earn some profit can enter his/her details(e.g time,venue,etc.) on the webapp.


Meanwhile,the other person in college who desires for a particular product from that particular area can place the order on the webapp. The person in the city will receive the order through text message.


A limit is specified on the number of orders.The deliverer will be paid by the buyer accordingly.(depending on the amount and no. of products).
